About Prajogo Tio

I am student at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, under the generous Nanyang Scholarship. I am enrolled in a scholars programme called Renaissance Engineering Programme, in which I am currently pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Engineering (B. Eng. Sc. Computer Engineering) and a Master's Degree in Technological Management (M. Sc. Technological Management).

My interests are in web and mobile apps development (both front-end and back-end), software engineering, enterprise-scale software development, algorithm and data structure research, and UI/UX design. I have a keen eye in design patterns, intuitive and elegant design, and solving difficult development problems by applying good design principles, efficient algorithms, and data structures.

I frequently join competitive programming events to deepen my knowledge and understanding on algorithms and data structures. I love to spend my time developing side projects in game programming, graphics programming, web apps development and design, and anything that catch my attention. I love hackathon experiences.

As I have background in hardcore, true-blue engineering disciplines (electrical, chemical, mechanical, civil and power engineering), I have strong mathematical and physics foundations. I am also trained in the business and management sides with background in corporate finance, accountancy, and business law.
